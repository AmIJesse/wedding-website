How we met

It all started with a spark—in 2021, when two electricians’ paths crossed on a job site. Little did we know, this job would be the beginning of a lifelong connection. As we worked together, it quickly became clear that our chemistry wasn’t just professional. In fact, we spent so much time joking around and enjoying each other's company that we might have taken a bit longer on the job than we originally planned!
From that first job, our bond grew stronger. By 2022, what began as a professional relationship turned into something much more meaningful—dating.
We’ve tackled numerous projects together, and through it all, our love has been the steady force that keeps us going. Our journey has been filled with laughter, late-night work sessions, and the joy of coming home to our two adorable dogs who keep us grounded and entertained.
As we prepare to light up the future together, we’re grateful for the sparks that brought us together and the love that continues to brighten our lives.
